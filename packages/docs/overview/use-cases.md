# Use Cases

Clarity Optimistic Oracle is infrastructure. Any protocol on Stacks can use it as a source of truth for verifiable claims.

## Prediction Markets

Resolve real-world outcomes on-chain — elections, sports, milestones — without a single point of failure.

> "The Kansas City Chiefs beat the Philadelphia Eagles in Super Bowl LVII"

## DeFi

Settle any condition — price thresholds, liquidation events, option expirations — using general-purpose data instead of narrow price feeds.

> "BTC/USD was below $55,000 at 00:00 UTC on March 15, 2024 per Coinbase Pro OHLCV"

## Cross-Chain Verification

Verify that a transaction on Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Solana actually happened, without trusting a centralized relayer.

> "Address 0xA1b2...F3d4 sent 1.0 ETH on Ethereum mainnet at block 19,204,811"

## Insurance and Risk

Trigger payouts based on verified real-world events: flight delays, weather data, protocol exploits.

> "Singapore Airlines flight SQ321 was delayed more than 3 hours on March 12, 2024"

## Real-World Assets

Verify off-chain valuations, ownership transfers, or compliance checks for tokenized yields.

> "U.S. Treasury 6-month T-bill yield was 5.27% at auction on March 11, 2024"
