# Roadmap

- **Become the truth layer for every app on Stacks** — COO is infrastructure. The goal is to be the default layer that every bridge, prediction market, insurance protocol, and DeFi app on Stacks plugs into. One protocol, powering an entire ecosystem.
- **Open to anyone** — We start with a controlled arbitration layer for reliability, with a clear path to fully decentralized resolution via token-weighted voting — anyone holding the governance token gets a say. No gatekeepers, no trust required.
- **Automatic payouts** — Once a claim is settled, the money moves on its own. Insurance pays out, bridges release funds, prediction markets resolve — all without anyone pressing a button.
- **Bring Your Own Rules** — Every app has different stakes. COO lets any protocol plug in their own resolution rules — faster, stricter, or custom to their use case.
- **Protocol fees** — Every claim posted could carry a small fee. As usage grows — more bridges, more prediction markets, more DeFi apps — that fee compounds into a sustainable revenue stream.
